Governance

Three boards, each independent. The HoldCo board governs the operating and custody subsidiaries; OpCo and CustodyCo each have separate boards with audit and risk committees.

Three boards. Independent governance.

HoldCo Board
4orm Finance Holdings Inc.

Independent chair plus independent and inside directors. Audit committee, risk committee, and compensation & nominating committee. Governs the operating and custody subsidiaries.

OpCo Board
4orm Finance Operations Inc.

Separate board for the regulated marketplace, dealer, and settlement operations. Independent directors with capital markets and regulated marketplace experience. Reports to HoldCo.

CustodyCo Board
4orm Custody & Trust Co.

Separate board for the qualified custody entity. Provincial trust company governance standards. Independent directors with trust, custody, and fiduciary experience. Reports to HoldCo.

Technology Partner
KCS Capital Inc.

Independent technology and research firm. Develops the platform; does not operate it. Separately governed Canadian entity. Independence is structural, there is no cross-board membership between KCS and any 4orm entity.
