Privacy Policy.

How 4orm Finance Holdings Inc. collects, uses, and protects the personal information of visitors, prospective counterparties, and access-requestors.

1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to the websites operated by 4orm Finance Holdings Inc. ("4orm Finance," "we," "us," "our"), including 4ormfinance.com and its subdomains, 4ormex.com, and 4ormedc.com. 4orm Finance is a Canadian corporation with offices in Calgary, Vancouver, and Edmonton.

2. Information we collect

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Request access to the 4orm Finance data room (name, email address, firm, phone number, investor classification, allocation range, timeline, and area of interest).
  • Contact us through our websites or by email.
  • Subscribe to research, briefs, or updates.

We also collect limited technical information automatically when you visit our websites, including IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and referring URL. We use first-party analytics; we do not sell visitor data.

3. How we use information

We use personal information to:

  • Review access requests to the data room and arrange introductory conversations.
  • Communicate with prospective institutional and accredited counterparties.
  • Comply with applicable Canadian securities laws and regulatory expectations.
  • Maintain and improve our websites.

4. Disclosure of information

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may disclose information to:

  • Service providers acting on our behalf under contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal, financial, and accounting professionals as required for the operation of the firm.
  • Canadian regulatory authorities and law enforcement as required by applicable law.

5. Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out above, or as required by applicable Canadian law.

6. Your rights

Subject to applicable Canadian privacy legislation (including PIPEDA and any applicable provincial private-sector privacy law), you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting compliance@4ormfinance.com.

7. Security

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information against loss, theft, and unauthorized access, use, disclosure, copying, or modification.

8. Cross-border transfers

Personal information may be processed in Canada and, where service providers operate cross-border, in other jurisdictions. We require such processors to maintain protections comparable to those required under Canadian law.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted to this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
